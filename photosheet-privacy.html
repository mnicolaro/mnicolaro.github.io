Photo Sheet Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Photo Sheet (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. User-Provided Content

  • Photos and images you upload or select
  • Captions, notes, markup, and photo sheet content
  • Generated PDF photo sheets

b. Device & Metadata

  • Image metadata, such as date, time, location, file name, and device details if available
  • Device type and basic usage data

c. Contact Information

  • Email address when sending photo sheets or contacting support

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create, display, export, and print photo sheets
  • Add captions, markup, notes, and optional metadata to photo sheets
  • Send emails on your behalf, such as photo sheets or exported PDFs
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Retention

Images, generated photo sheets, PDFs, captions, markup, and related content may be stored temporarily. All uploaded content is automatically deleted within 7 days of creation. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is securely stored.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our app:

  • Firebase by Google for storage and backend services
  • Apple services for app functionality, sharing, printing, and device permissions

These providers may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Your content may be shared:

  • When you export, print, email, or otherwise share a photo sheet
  • With recipients you choose when sending photo sheets or PDFs

7. Notifications

If enabled, we may send notifications related to:

  • Photo sheet creation, export, or sharing activity
  • App updates or important account/service notices

You can disable notifications in your device settings.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Stop using the app at any time, which stops further data collection

To request data deletion, contact us at: FinnsMedia@nicolaro.com

9. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our website.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: FinnsMedia@nicolaro.com
